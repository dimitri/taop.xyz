Why fair pricing?

Pricing should reflect your situation. If you're in a region with lower purchasing power, or facing budget constraints, you shouldn't be locked out of knowledge. If you can afford the standard price, please choose it — it sustains this work and keeps it accessible for others.

Fair pricing isn't about getting the cheapest option. It's about honesty: you decide what the book is worth to you, based on what you learn from it and what you can afford.
